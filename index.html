SubAdventure's Spots

CANCUN

Speedboat adventure and snorkeling in Cancun. Sail your own boat through picturesque mangrove jungle canals and out to sea.

MALDIVES

The fauna is exuberant. Back Faru: this site offers a beautiful drift dive along the fringing reef. Often protected, it's a pleasant dive, ideal for a first contact with the underwater world of the Maldives. Sharks, barracudas, eagle rays and turtles are a regular surprise.

NINGALOO

The Ningaloo Reef is approximately 260km long and is one of the largest fringing reefs in the world! It supports an abundance of fish (500 species), hard and soft corals (300 species), molluscs (600 species) and many other invertebrates.

PUNTA CANA

Explore diving in Punta Cana Punta Cana's coastline is home to the country's longest reef, stretching north to Bavaro. Here you'll find an exciting variety of diving environments, from caves and canyons to shallow reefs and wrecks.
